Cerys Atchison, MRTPI is a dedicated and experienced Town Planner with a strong background in urban development, planning policy, and sustainable land use. As a Member of the Royal Town Planning Institute (MRTPI), she brings expertise in navigating complex planning regulations, stakeholder engagement, and strategic place-making.

Currently working in the planning sector, Cerys is passionate about creating well-designed, inclusive communities and ensuring development aligns with long-term environmental and social goals. With excellent analytical and communication skills, she collaborates effectively with councils, developers, and local communities to deliver balanced and forward-thinking planning solutions.

Cerys holds a Master's degree in Town Planning and has worked on a variety of projects from small-scale residential developments to large urban regeneration schemes. Her approach combines technical knowledge with a commitment to public service, ensuring that planning decisions reflect the needs and aspirations of local residents while promoting sustainable growth
