Riverside is on the brink of an exciting transformation! Our Town Planning Initiative is a carefully designed project that prioritizes accessibility, sustainability, and a stronger sense of community. Over the next few years, residents will see wider sidewalks, ADA-compliant crossings, and upgraded public transit stops—making it easier for everyone, including seniors and people with disabilities, to navigate our downtown. But that’s just the beginning. The plan introduces mixed-use developments that blend residential, retail, and green spaces, reducing urban sprawl and fostering a walkable lifestyle. New pocket parks and pedestrian plazas will offer gathering spots for farmers’ markets, festivals, and everyday relaxation. Meanwhile, energy-efficient street lighting, solar-powered charging stations, and expanded bike lanes will help Riverside cut its carbon footprint while keeping the town modern and connected. This isn’t just about infrastructure—it’s about people. Affordable housing provisions ensure long-time residents aren’t priced out, while new small business incentives will bring fresh energy to our local economy. We want Riverside to be a place where families, young professionals, and retirees all feel at home. Have ideas or concerns? Join us at the next community workshop or share feedback online. Together, we can shape a Riverside that works for everyone.

Maplewood’s heart is getting a major upgrade! The Core District Revitalization Project is a multi-year effort to modernize our downtown, making it more attractive to visitors, shoppers, and entrepreneurs. Gone are the days of congested streets and underused storefronts—instead, imagine wider sidewalks with café seating, smart parking systems that reduce traffic, and beautifully restored historic buildings housing boutique shops and tech-friendly workspaces. A key focus is supporting local businesses. The plan includes grants for façade improvements, pop-up market spaces for artisans, and streamlined permitting for new ventures. We’re also introducing free public Wi-Fi and upgraded event spaces to draw more foot traffic year-round, from summer concert series to winter holiday markets. For residents, the benefits go beyond shopping. New loft-style apartments above retail spaces will bring more life to the district after hours, while green infrastructure—like rain gardens and shade trees—will keep the area cool and sustainable. Studies show that vibrant downtowns increase property values and community pride, and we’re committed to making Maplewood a regional destination. Let’s build this together! Attend a planning session or follow our progress online. The new Maplewood isn’t just a dream—it’s in the works.

Families in Havenbrook deserve a town that grows with them—and that’s exactly what the Havenbrook Enhancement Project will deliver. This comprehensive plan focuses on sustainability, safety, and spaces that bring neighbors together. Picture tree-lined streets with reduced speed limits, new crosswalks near schools, and interconnected bike paths that make it easy (and safe) for kids to ride to the park. Green spaces take center stage. Over a dozen underutilized lots will be converted into pocket parks, community gardens, and rain gardens that manage stormwater naturally. A brand-new central plaza will host outdoor movies, seasonal markets, and live music, while a modernized community center will offer after-school programs, fitness classes, and meeting rooms for local groups. Sustainability isn’t an afterthought—it’s built into every detail. Solar panels on municipal buildings, LED streetlights, and incentives for eco-friendly home upgrades will shrink Havenbrook’s environmental impact. Plus, the plan preserves the town’s small-town charm by prioritizing infill development over sprawl, keeping our neighborhoods tight-knit and our green belts intact. This is your Havenbrook—let’s make it even better. Share your input at our open houses or online. Together, we’re creating a legacy for future generations.
